Relational Logic & OwnershipTo fulfill the requirement of knowing "Who owns the hospital" and "What specialties are available," we need to expand the registration payload.The "Hospital Profile" LogicWhen a hospital registers, they aren't just an address; they are a legal entity. We will track the Owner/Authorized Signatory to ensure accountability.The "Staff Inventory" LogicInstead of just a count (e.g., "10 doctors"), we will store individual doctor profiles. This allows the Phase 2 "Keyword Search" to actually find a person, not just a building.2. Updated Database Schema (Neon DB)We will add two primary layers to the existing Postgres schema.A.
